#**Bold Move Foundation Website**

**Overview**
This website was developed for the Bold Move Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable communities in Kenya through education, healthcare, economic empowerment, digital literacy, and humanitarian support.

The website serves as an online platform to raise awareness about the foundation’s mission, objectives, team, and community impact while providing visitors with ways to connect and support its initiatives.

**Features**

Responsive website design
Home page introducing the foundation’s mission
About Us section
Objectives section
Team page
Contact page
Image gallery and visual content
Easy navigation across pages

**Technologies Used**

HTML5
CSS3
JavaScript
GitHub Pages (for deployment)

**Website Structure**

index.html – Home page
about.html – Information about the foundation
objectives.html – Foundation goals and objectives
team.html – Team members and leadership
contact.html – Contact information and inquiries
css/ – Stylesheets
images/ – Website images and media

**Live Website**

https://hermimab.github.io/Bold-Move-Foundation/

**Purpose**

The purpose of this website is to provide an accessible and engaging digital presence for the Bold Move Foundation, helping communicate its mission, showcase its initiatives, and connect with supporters, partners, and beneficiaries.
